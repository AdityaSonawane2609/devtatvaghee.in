How It's Made

From Cow to Kitchen —
The Bilona Way

01

Fresh Milk Collected

Pure A2 milk collected daily from our indigenous Gir cows, grazed freely on open natural pastures in Maharashtra.

02

Boiled & Set into Curd

Milk is gently boiled and cultured overnight into thick, creamy makhan — the living foundation of real ghee.

03

Hand-Churned with Wooden Bilona

Makkhan is slowly hand-churned using a traditional wooden bilona, separating pure butter exactly as done for thousands of years.

04

Slow-Cooked to Golden Ghee

Butter is gently simmered on low heat until it transforms into pure, aromatic, golden A2 ghee — then carefully jarred with love.
